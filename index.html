IDLI

Idli is a type of savoury rice cake served with sambhar, a lentil based vegetable curry,cooked with pegion pea and tamarind broth, along with a variety of chutneys which are commonly called as indian sauces.The cakes are made by steaming a batter consisting of fermented black lentils (de-husked) and rice.Idli is a type of savoury rice cake served with sambhar, a lentil based vegetable curry,cooked with pegion pea and tamarind broth, along with a variety of chutneys which are commonly called as indian sauces.The cakes are made by steaming a batter consisting of fermented black lentils (de-husked) and rice.Idli is a type of savoury rice cake served with sambhar, a lentil based vegetable curry,cooked with pegion pea and tamarind broth, along with a variety of chutneys which are commonly called as indian sauces.The cakes are made by steaming a batter consisting of fermented black lentils (de-husked) and rice.Idli is a type of savoury rice cake served with sambhar, a lentil based vegetable curry,cooked with pegion pea and tamarind broth, along with a variety of chutneys which are commonly called as indian sauces.The cakes are made by steaming a batter consisting of fermented black lentils (de-husked) and rice.(Back to top)
